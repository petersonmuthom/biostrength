About us

We focus on supporting the development of sustainable clinical and research laboratory systems that generate accurate, reliable, and timely data. These systems play a central role in patient care, disease prevention and control, drug and vaccine development, and evidence-based public healthdecision-making.

In many resource-constrained settings, laboratory services continue to face challenges related to quality, timeliness, and reliability. Biostrength Kenya Limited addresses these gaps by promoting skills development, leadership strengthening, and standards-aligned practices that enhance laboratory performance.

We recognize that effective laboratory systems depend on competent personnel, strong governance, and policies that comply with internationally accepted regulations. Through training, capacity building, and digital learning solutions, Biostrength Kenya Limited works to strengthen laboratory systems that support resilient healthcare systems and improved public health outcomes.
